What this is

Mise en Scroll is a side project I built for myself — a single place to browse new posts from the food blogs I actually follow, without jumping between tabs or relying on social media algorithms to surface them.

Every card links back to the original post. I don't host any content — just the title, date, thumbnail, and a short excerpt pulled from each blog's public RSS feed.

A note on content ownership

All recipes, photos, and writing belong to their original authors. I'm not claiming any of it — this is just a reading tool, the same way a podcast app doesn't own the podcasts.

RSS feeds exist specifically so readers can follow a site through third-party tools. That's the intended use, and it's all this does.

Want your blog removed?

No problem at all. Open a GitHub issue or email mise.en.scroll.app@gmail.com and I'll take it down within 48 hours.

No ads, no tracking

This is a personal project. No advertising, no affiliate links, no analytics, no monetisation of any kind.
